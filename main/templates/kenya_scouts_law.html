Kenya Scouts Law and Structure

The Kenya Scouts Association follows a structured organization that promotes leadership, character development, and community service. Our structure is designed to support scouts at all levels, from young members to adult leaders.

As part of the Kasarani Scouts Local Association, we adhere to the principles and laws established by the Kenya Scouts Association, which guide our activities and interactions within the scouting community.
