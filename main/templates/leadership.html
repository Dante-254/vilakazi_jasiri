Structure

Leadership/Crew Council

Our leadership structure is built on the principles of servant leadership and democratic decision-making. The Crew Council consists of elected members who represent the interests of all crew members and work together to plan activities and make important decisions.

The leadership team is responsible for organizing events, managing resources, and ensuring that all members have opportunities to grow and contribute to the crew's mission.
