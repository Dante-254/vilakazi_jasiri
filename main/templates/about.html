Created on 2015 Villakazi Rover Crew is a group of young individuals committed to making a positive impact in our community through service, leadership, and personal development. As part of the Kasarani Scouts Local Association Which is part to the Kenya Scouts Association, we engage in various activities that promote social welfare, environmental conservation, and youth empowerment.

Our mission is to help young people develop life skills, character, and leadership through fun, challenging outdoor and community-based activities like camping, hiking, and volunteering, focusing on "learning by doing" to build confidence, teamwork, and a sense of responsibility. They earn badges for mastering new skills, from survival to STEM, while exploring their interests and making a positive impact.
